# Piece Moment App Store Copy

## Positioning

Piece Moment is a cozy jigsaw puzzle app for antistress short breaks.

The core feeling is not competition or speed. It is opening the app during travel, waiting, bedtime, or any small break, choosing a pleasant picture, and spending a few calm minutes putting it back together. Personal photos and children's drawings are part of that promise: everyday images can also become small playable moments.

## English Metadata

### App Name

Piece Moment: Cozy Jigsaw

### Subtitle

Antistress puzzles for breaks

### Promotional Text

Choose a picture, relax for a moment, and enjoy the small satisfaction of putting it together at your own pace.

### Description

Piece Moment is a relaxing jigsaw puzzle app for the small quiet spaces in your day.

Browse gentle image collections, choose a picture that fits your mood, and turn it into a puzzle you can enjoy at your own pace. Pick an easy or challenging grid, move each piece without pressure, and enjoy the calm reward of watching the image come back together.

You can also bring in your own photos from your album. A child's drawing, a favorite meal, a travel snapshot, or a simple everyday scene can become a puzzle you return to for a few peaceful minutes.

Features:

- Open anytime with no ads and no network required
- Relaxing jigsaw gameplay with multiple difficulty levels
- Comfortable image browsing across themed collections
- Support for personal photos from your album
- Optional puzzle titles and notes that appear after completion
- Gentle progress, stars, unlocks, and recommendations
- Clean visual design made for focused, unhurried play

Take a little Piece Moment. One picture at a time.
