Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Piece Moment is designed to be calm — and that calm extends to how it treats your privacy: we don't collect your personal data, we don't show ads, and the app works fully offline.

Information we collect

None. Piece Moment does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not collect analytics or usage tracking. We have no servers that store your information.

Your photos stay on your device

When you turn a personal photo into a puzzle, the image is read from your photo library and processed entirely on your device. It is never uploaded to us or anyone else. Granting photo access is optional and only used to let you pick a picture.

No network required

Normal play needs no internet connection. The app does not send your gameplay, photos, or any personal data over the network.

Children

Piece Moment is family-friendly: no ads, no purchases that target children, and no data collection — making it safe for players of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update this page. Since we don't collect contact information, please check back here for the latest version.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at keyipeng@gmail.com.
